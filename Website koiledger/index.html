Why KOI Ledger?

The Japanese koi industry is built on trust, pedigree, and authenticity. Today most records are paper-based and easily lost or forged. KOI Ledger creates an immutable, verifiable digital identity for every premium koi fish.

Core Features

Digital Passport

Immutable lineage, breeder details, and photos stored on XRPL

Ownership Transfer

Secure ownership change linked with XRPL payments

Health Records

Vaccination, growth and treatment logs

QR Verification

Instant authenticity check for buyers and auctions
