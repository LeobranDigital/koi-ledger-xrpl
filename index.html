KOI Ledger

Digital Passport for Premium Koi (Nishikigoi) on XRPL

A trusted blockchain platform that verifies lineage, ownership, health records, and authenticity of high-value koi with secure XRPL payments.

The Industry Problem

Fake Lineage

Pedigree papers are easily forged, reducing buyer confidence.

Opaque Ownership

No global standard to verify previous owners or auction history.

Risky Payments

International sales rely on manual transfers with high fraud risk.

Our Solution

KOI Ledger creates a tamper-proof digital passport for every Nishikigoi. Each fish receives a unique ID linked to breeder, lineage, medical records, ownership transfers, and authenticated images – all anchored on the XRP Ledger.

  • Immutable provenance and pedigree verification
  • Secure XRPL-based JPY/XRP payments
  • Transparent transfer of ownership
  • QR-code public verification for auctions and buyers

Powered by XRPL

Blockchain Capabilities

  • Transaction memos store koi metadata hash
  • Fast, low-cost settlement in seconds
  • Escrow for safe auctions
  • Multi-currency payments (JPY/XRP/RLUSD)

What Goes On-Chain

  • Koi unique identifier
  • Ownership transfer events
  • Hash of health and pedigree records
  • Verified payment proof

How the Demo Works

Register Koi & Mint Passport
Attach Lineage & Health Data
Execute XRPL Payment
Transfer Ownership On-Chain

Judges can scan a QR code to instantly verify authenticity on the public ledger.
